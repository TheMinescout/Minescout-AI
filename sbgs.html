Sammamish Botanical Garden Society

Growing places where Sammamish slows down, gathers, and blooms.

SBGS is a volunteer-powered nonprofit working with the City of Sammamish to steward the gardens of Big Rock Park and the Lower Commons. We blend local pride, year-round care, and hands-in-the-soil action that keeps community spaces vibrant.
